I keep notes because I forget details quickly, not because the system itself is interesting.

The setup that works best for me is intentionally plain: a small inbox, a few durable folders, and a habit of rewriting rough notes into something I would actually want to find later.

Good notes reduce the number of decisions I need to make twice. They do not need to be clever to be useful.
