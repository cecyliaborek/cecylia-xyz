I'm a generalist: lots of interests, never quite enough time for all of them. I write this site in English and Polish about tech, everyday life, and culture — observations, things I'm learning, and whatever else I want to think through in public.

## Work

Currently I work at [Splunk](https://www.splunk.com/), building tools for other developers. As a true generalist, I don't have favorite technology or area of expertise. I take the biggest satisfaction from learning new things.

Before Splunk, I worked at several companies across Europe on a wide range of projects. _True generalist_. From enhancing 4G connectivity at Nokia, through improving compatibility for millions of Windows users, to developing an emergency management application at [eurofunk Kappacher](https://www.eurofunk.com/).
